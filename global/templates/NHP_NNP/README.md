# NHP_NNP Brain Template & Atlas Collection

## NHP brain template and atlas
 - This folder collects all the templates and atlases that are useful for resolving cortical folding, parcellation, and connectivity in various mammalian species.
 - All the data was preprocessed with HCP pipeline, dedrifted (or unbiased) for population-wise non-linear warping distortion.
 - Macaque data was collected from RIKEN Center for Biosystems Dynacmics Reseach (BDR) in Kobe, Japan, and Stem-cell and Brain Research Institute (SBRI) in Lyon, France, while marmoset data from RIKEN BDR, and Chimpanzee data from Yerkes National Primate Research Center (YNPRC) in Atlanta, USA. Data acquisition method is described in README in each species,
 - Currently available species are chimpanzee, rhesus macaque, cynomolgus macaque, and marmoset. Snow monkey and night monkey are also planned. Rodents are also under consideration
