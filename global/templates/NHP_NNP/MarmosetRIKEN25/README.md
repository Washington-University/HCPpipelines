# Marmoset  brain templates - MarmosetRIKEN25

## Data
- Marmoset (*Callithrix jacchus*; n = 25; age = 4.9 ± 1.2 years; 
  female:male = 12:13) MRI scans were obtained as part of the international 
  initiative, the Non-Human Primate Neuroimaging & Neuroanatomy Project (NHP_NNP).
- All marmosets were housed at the RIKEN Center for Biosystems Dynamics 
  Research (BDR) in Kobe, Japan. All procedures were carried out in accordance 
  with protocols approved by the RIKEN Kobe Animal Care and Use Committee.
- T1w and T2w structural MRI were acquired at 0.36 mm isotropic resolution on a 
  Siemens 3T Prisma scanner using a Takashima Small Primate 16-channel multi-array 
  head coil. A scanning protocol is available at [NHP_NNP site](https://brainminds-beyond.riken.jp/hcp-nhp-protocol/).
- Data were collected, then preprocessed with the HCP pipeline, supported by 
  AMED grant JP18dm0307006 (T.H.), JSPS KAKENHI JP22H04926 (T.H.), Wellcome Trust
  (S.S.), and NIH R01 MH60974 (D.V.E., M.F.G.).
- Data were publicly released as part of AMED grant JP23wm0625001 (T.H.) and
  NIH grant 1UM1MH130981-01 (E.L.).
